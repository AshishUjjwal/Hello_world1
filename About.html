About Us

Welcome to the Training and Placement Department of Ambalika Institute of Management and Technology! In the short span of 10 years since its establishment, AIMT has built a reputation as an institution par excellence dedicated to the holistic development of students with a strong foundation of experiential learning, including scientific, technical, and soft skill training. The Training and Placement Department is an integral part of the institute, where activities are organized throughout the year in an effort to prepare prospective students for the campus selection programs. AIMT believes in following the essence of the definition of Engineering by training students to make the potential value of science a reality by translating scientific knowledge into tools. What set us apart are our approach and the mechanism, which reflect our competence and farsightedness.
